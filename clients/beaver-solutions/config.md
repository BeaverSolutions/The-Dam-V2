## Outreach Playbook Notes

**Best performing angles:**
- Lead with a specific observation about their company (not a generic compliment)
- Reference the founder-doing-all-sales pain directly — it resonates immediately
- Keep it founder-to-founder: "I built this because I had the same problem"
- Malaysian context works: reference KL, Klang Valley, local companies

**Avoid:**
- Mentioning "AI" in the subject line — often triggers spam or scepticism
- Lengthy first emails — if it needs scrolling, it's too long
- Formal sign-offs like "Best regards" — use "Cheers" or just the name
- Asking for a demo as the first CTA — too much commitment too early

**First message structure:**
1. Specific observation — something real about their company or role
2. One-line pain bridge — "Most founders I talk to are doing all their own sales"
3. One-line value prop — what we do in plain English
4. Soft CTA — "Worth a 20-min chat?"
